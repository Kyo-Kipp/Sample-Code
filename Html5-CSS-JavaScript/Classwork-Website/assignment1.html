How to Bake A Cake


Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Flour

  • 4 eggs

  • 2 sticks of butter

  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar

  • 1 tablespoon of Vanilla extract

  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

  • 3/4 cup of Milk

  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 350 Degrees F.

  2. combine flour, baking powder, and salt together in seperate bowl.

  3. beat butter and sugar in large bowl or mixer until light and fluffy.

  4. Add eggs to mixer one at a time and then Add Vanilla.

  5. Add the Flour mixture slowly alternating milk and flour until smooth.

  6. Pour your batter into your pan or pans.

  7. Bake aproxamately 25-30 minutes until golden on top.(beware of Altitude cooking!)

  8. Remove from oven and transfer to Cooling Racks. Let sit for aproxamately 10 min.

  9. Run a spatula around edge of pan and flip upside-down to let cool completely.

  10. Cover with desired Icing and enjoy!
